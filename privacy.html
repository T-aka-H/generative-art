Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2025

Overview

Gen-Art is a generative art application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

Data We Do Not Collect

Gen-Art does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No user accounts or registration
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No cookies or advertising identifiers
  • No server-side data storage
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data

Device Sensors

Gen-Art may request access to the following device sensors to enhance the interactive art experience:

  • Motion & Orientation — Accelerometer, gyroscope, and compass data are used to control visual effects such as wave direction, wind, and flow. This data is processed entirely on-device and is never transmitted or stored.
  • Location — Latitude may be used once to adjust the color palette (e.g., tropical vs. arctic tones). This data is processed on-device and is never transmitted or stored.

All sensor permissions are optional. The app functions fully without granting any sensor access.

Audio

Gen-Art generates ambient sound using the Web Audio API. All audio is synthesized on-device from mathematical functions. No microphone access is requested or used.

Third-Party Services

Gen-Art does not integrate any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Gen-Art does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
