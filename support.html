Frequently Asked Questions

The art does not respond to tilting or shaking.
When you first open a work, your device may ask for permission to use motion sensors. Please tap "Allow" to enable tilt and shake interactions. If you denied the permission, you can re-enable it in Settings > Safari (or the app name) > Motion & Orientation Access.
I don't hear any sound.
Sound is generated after your first touch on the screen. Make sure your device is not in silent mode and the volume is turned up. Sound is synthesized in real-time and requires a user interaction to start (a browser/OS requirement).
How do I navigate between works?
Tap the hamburger menu icon in the top-left corner of any work to return to the gallery. From the gallery, tap any preview to enter that work.
How do I change the time of day?
Tap the sky area (top 30% of the screen) to advance the color theme by 2 hours. Double-tap to return to the real-time clock.
What devices are supported?
Gen-Art is designed for iPhone. It uses device sensors (gyroscope, accelerometer, compass) for the full interactive experience. All visual features work on any device, but sensor-based interactions require hardware support.

System Requirements

iOS 14.0 or later.
